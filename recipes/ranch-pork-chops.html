Ranch Pork Chops Air Fried

In recent years air frying food has become common place. This recipe will take a common meal and add it to the great foods that can be air fried.

    Ingredients

  • 4 boneless. center-but pock chops, 1-inch thick
  • cooking spray
  • 2 teaspoons dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • aluminum foil

    Steps

  1. Place pork chops on a plate and lightly spray both slides with cooking spray. Sprinkle both sides with rach seasoning mix and let sit at room temperature for 10 minutes.
  2. Spray the basket of an air fryer with cooking spray and preheat the air fryer to 390 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  3. Place chops in the preheated air fryer, working in batches if necessary, to ensure fryer is not overcrowded.
  4. Cook for 5 minutes. Flip chops and cook 5 minutes more. Let rest on a foil-coverd plate for 5 minutes before serving.
